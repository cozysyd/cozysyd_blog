About Cozy Syd

Hi! My name is Sydney, and I'm 27 years old, currently living in San Diego, CA. I'm so happy you stopped by my little webpage!

I grew up in Massachusetts and from a young age, I've always had a wild imagination. My dad likes to tell me that my imagination was and still is my superpower. I've always loved creating stories in my head as well as consuming as many as I could in the form of books, movies, TV shows, and video games.

This grew into a love of storytelling, so I ended up going to college in North Carolina at Elon University and got a degree in Cinema and Television Arts. However, during the spring of 2018, my senior year, two reporters exposed decades of abuse that women in Hollywood have been forced to endure, and thus the Me Too movement burst on to the scene


Disilusioned by the film and TV industry, I decided to take the advice of a mentor and hold off on pursuing a job in that field. I took some time to find myself, did a few odd jobs, and ended up moving to Southern California during the pandemic. I decided to go back to school to figure out what I wanted to do next. Somewhere on this path I found myself in possession of an Nintendo Switch and decided to try out this game everyone was obsessing over called Animal Crossing


And then...Cozy Syd was born!
